How to Make Rock Candy

Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients

You'll need sugar, water, a jar, string, and food coloring (optional).

Step 2: Prepare the Sugar Solution

Boil water and dissolve sugar to create a supersaturated solution.

Step 3: Set Up the Crystallization Process

Dip your string into the solution and let it rest in a jar to grow crystals.

Step 4: Wait and Observe

After several days, the crystals will grow larger. Enjoy your homemade rock candy!
